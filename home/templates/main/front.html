Setsuhi recently had the honor of calligraphing the emblem to appear on the jerseys of Japan's National Football Team for the 2014 FIFA World Cup in Brazil! The emblem is the word 円陣 (“enjin”), meaning “huddle”. In these photographs which were shared by an Adidas employee: the official jersey with the emblem on its left sleeve; employees at the Adidas Group form an enjin; and the enjin emblem can be seen in various places around the Adidas Group's office. For more on the concept behind “円陣” please check out Adidas's {% link "article about the jersey" %}.
